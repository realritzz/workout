Upper body · Push
Push day
Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
Pushing movements — chest, front shoulders, back of arms. All machine and cable based so form is guided for you.
🎯 How to pick your weight
Too easy
add weight
Working
target zone
Form breaks
reduce weight
Last 2–3 reps should feel hard but your form stays clean. That's your working weight. Write it down and add a little each week.
🔥 Warmup: 5 min treadmill walk · 10 arm circles each direction · 10 shoulder rolls
Exercises
🏋️
1Machine chest press
3 sets10–12 reps60s rest
Seat height: handles at mid-chest. Push straight out, don't flare elbows too wide. If you can do 12 easily, go heavier next set.
🏋️
2Seated dumbbell shoulder press
3 sets10–12 reps60s rest
Start light. Press straight up overhead, don't arch your lower back. Back flat on the pad throughout.
🏋️
3Cable chest fly (low pulley)
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
Slight bend in elbows throughout. Squeeze at center like hugging a tree. Cables at the bottom, sweep upward and together.
🏋️
4Lateral raises (dumbbells)
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
Raise only to shoulder height. Lead with elbows, not hands. Go lighter than you think — these isolate a small muscle.
🏋️
5Tricep pushdown (cable, rope)
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
Keep elbows glued to your sides. Push down and flare the rope apart at the bottom. Full extension every rep.
❄️ Cooldown: 5 min walk · Doorway chest stretch 30s each side
📋 Ground rules
🥩
Protein first
0.7–1g per lb of goal bodyweight daily. Eggs, chicken, Greek yogurt. Hit this before anything else.
📈
Progressive overload
Add a little weight or a rep every 1–2 weeks. That's how you keep getting stronger.
😴
Sleep = growth
Muscle is built during sleep. 7–9 hours is part of the program, not optional.
💧
Hydration
80–100oz of water daily. More on workout days. Dehydration kills performance.
🔁
Consistency wins
Missed a day? Pick back up. Two months of 80% beats one perfect week every time.
🚫
Don't skip legs
Leg day burns the most calories and releases the most growth hormone. It matters.
Upper body · Pull
Pull day
Back · Biceps · Rear delts
Pulling movements build back width, posture, and arm thickness. Cables and dumbbells.
🔥 Warmup: 5 min treadmill · 10 arm swings · Cat-cow stretch 10 reps
Exercises
🏋️
1Lat pulldown (cable machine)
3 sets10–12 reps60s rest
Pull bar to upper chest, lean back slightly. Imagine pulling your elbows down to your hips — not yanking with your arms.
🏋️
2Seated cable row
3 sets10–12 reps60s rest
Sit tall, pull handle to belly button. Squeeze shoulder blades together at the end. Don't round your back on the return.
🏋️
3Dumbbell bicep curl
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
No swinging. 3 seconds on the way down. Keep elbows at your sides throughout.
🏋️
4Face pulls (cable, rope at eye level)
3 sets15 reps45s rest
Pull rope to your face, elbows flare outward. Great for posture and shoulder health. Don't skip this one.
🏋️
5Dumbbell shrugs
3 sets15 reps45s rest
Straight up and down — no rolling. Hold at the top for 1 second. Heavy is fine here.
❄️ Cooldown: 5 min walk · Cross-body shoulder stretch 30s each arm · Lat stretch

Lower body
Leg day
Quads · Hamstrings · Glutes · Calves
Legs are your biggest muscle group — this burns the most calories and releases the most growth hormone. Don't skip it.
🔥 Warmup: 5 min bike or treadmill · 10 bodyweight squats · 10 leg swings each side
Exercises
🏋️
1Leg press (machine)
3 sets12–15 reps60s rest
Feet shoulder width, mid-platform. Don't lock knees at the top. Start lighter than you think — range of motion matters more than weight.
🏋️
2Leg extension (machine)
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
Isolates your quads. Controlled — don't kick up fast. Slow on the way down. Full extension at the top.
🏋️
3Leg curl (seated or lying machine)
3 sets12–15 reps45s rest
Hamstrings. Curl all the way through, squeeze at full contraction. Slow and controlled on the return.
🏋️
4Hip abduction machine
3 sets15 reps45s rest
Push outward against the pads. Great for glutes and hip stability. Don't just throw legs out — feel the squeeze at the top.
🏋️
5Calf raises (machine or standing)
3 sets20 reps30s rest
Full range of motion — all the way up, all the way down. Slow descent. Calves respond best to high reps and full stretch.
❄️ Cooldown: 5 min walk · Quad stretch 30s each · Seated hamstring stretch 45s each side
Full body
Full body / Core
Compound moves · Abs · Stability
Compounds hit everything at once. Core work is invisible when you have it and obvious when you don't.
🔥 Warmup: 5 min walk · Hip circles · Arm swings · 5 inchworms
Exercises
🏋️
1Goblet squat (dumbbell)
3 sets12 reps60s rest
Hold one dumbbell at chest height. Squat deep, chest up, knees track over toes. Elbows inside your knees at the bottom.
🏋️
2Dumbbell Romanian deadlift
3 sets12 reps60s rest
Hinge at hips, soft knees. Feel the stretch in your hamstrings. Flat back always. Dumbbells stay close to your legs throughout.
🏋️
3Cable woodchop (high to low)
3 sets12 each side45s rest
Cable set high, pull diagonally down across your body. Pivot your back foot. Control the return — don't let the cable pull you.
🏋️
4Plank (forearm)
3 sets20–40s hold30s rest
Elbows under shoulders, squeeze glutes and core. Don't let hips sag or rise. Breathe steadily. Add 5 seconds per week.
🏋️
5Hanging knee raises (captain's chair)
3 sets10–15 reps45s rest
Slow and controlled — don't swing. Bring knees to chest, lower slowly. The slow lower is where the work happens.
❄️ Cooldown: 5 min walk · Full body stretch routine
Cardio
Cardio day
Endurance · Fat burn · Active
Cardio accelerates fat loss without eating into muscle when kept moderate. 30–45 min is the sweet spot.
Pick one — 30–45 min
🏃 Treadmill incline walk
30–40 min at 3.5–4mph, 8–12% incline. Low impact, high fat burn. Your existing walking habit — just steeper.
🔄 Elliptical
30–40 min moderate pace. Easy on joints, full body. Good option if legs are sore from Thursday.
🚴 Stationary bike
30 min steady, or 20 min intervals: 1 min hard / 2 min easy × 7. Intervals burn more in less time.
🌳 Outdoor walk / jog
Your existing habit extended to 45–60 min. Add jogging intervals when you're ready.

Eat well · Sleep · Reset
