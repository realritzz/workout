Upper body · Push
Monday — Push Day
Chest · Front Shoulders · Triceps
All pushing movements. Machines first, then cables, then dumbbells — stay in your zone and minimize walking.

Upper body · Pull
Tuesday — Pull Day
Back · Biceps · Rear Delts · Traps
All pulling movements. Cables first, then machines, then dumbbells — stay clustered.

3 sets15 reps45s rest

Cardio
Saturday — Cardio Day
Endurance · Fat burn · Active recovery
30–45 min steady cardio. Accelerates fat loss without eating into muscle when kept moderate.
Pick one — 30–45 min
🏃 Treadmill incline walk
30–40 min at 3.5–4mph, 8–12% incline. Low impact, high fat burn. Your existing habit — just steeper.
🔄 Elliptical
30–40 min moderate pace. Easy on joints, full body movement. Good if legs are still sore from Thursday.
🚴 Stationary bike
30 min steady, or 20 min intervals: 1 min hard / 2 min easy × 7. Intervals burn more in less time.
🌳 Outdoor walk / jog
Your existing habit extended to 45–60 min. Add jogging intervals when you're ready to progress.
Rest
Sunday — Full Rest
Eat well · Sleep · Reset
Complete recovery. You don't build muscle while training — you build it while resting.
Today's goals
  • 🍳Meal prep — protein-first meals ready for the week saves every day
  • 🚶Light walk if you feel like it — never forced
  • 🌙7–9 hours sleep — this is part of the program
  • 💧Hydrate — 80–100oz of water today
📋 Ground rules
🥩
Protein first
0.7–1g per lb of goal bodyweight daily. Eggs, chicken, Greek yogurt, ground turkey.
📈
Progressive overload
Add a rep or a little weight every 1–2 weeks. Log it below every exercise so you know.
😴
Sleep = growth
7–9 hours. Muscle is built during sleep. Non-negotiable.
💧
Hydration
80–100oz daily. More on gym days. Dehydration kills performance fast.
🔁
Consistency wins
Missed a day? Pick back up. Two months of 80% beats one perfect week.
🚫
Don't skip legs
Leg day burns the most calories and releases the most growth hormone. It matters.
